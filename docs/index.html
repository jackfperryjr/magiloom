MAGILOOM

// features

Everything you need in Elanthia

Built for real play — not just a thin wrapper around the raw telnet stream.

⚡

Real-Time XML Parsing

Full DragonRealms XML stream parser. Vitals, roundtime, room data, active spells, indicators — all updated live as you play.

▦

Resizable Panel Layout

Drag panels to any height from the top or bottom edge. Slide the divider between game output and sidebar left or right. Your layout is saved automatically.

✦

Custom Highlights

Regex or plain-text patterns with custom foreground, background, and bold styling. Spot your character name, loot, danger, and more at a glance.

⚙

Lich Scripting

Launch Lich straight from the login screen. Script output is captured in a separate log drawer so it doesn't clutter your game output.

◈

Six Themes

Default, Bloodstone, Thornwood, Parchment, and Discord — or Final Fantasy IV. Each with a full color palette for health, mana, speech, warnings, and more.

↻

Auto-Updates

New releases download silently in the background. A banner appears when the update is ready — no manual downloads or reinstalls needed.

// panels

Your character, at a glance

An always-on vitals bar up top, plus panels for your current room, experience, active spells, conversation log (with player avatars), combat stream, atmosphere, deaths, and inventory — each panel you can show, hide, and resize.

// getting started

Up and running in minutes

No config files to edit. Download, install, log in with your DragonRealms account, pick your character, and you're in.

  1. 01 Download and install MAGILOOM for your platform.
  2. 02 Enter your DragonRealms account credentials on the login screen.
  3. 03 Select your character and connect. Your layout and highlights are saved automatically.
  4. 04 Optional: Add your Lich path in Settings to enable scripting.
